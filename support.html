How it works

Point your camera at a restaurant receipt. DipOut reads the subtotal, tax, and printed tip suggestions, then checks whether the restaurant calculated those tips on the pre-tax or post-tax total. If they tipped on the tax, it recalculates a fair tip on the subtotal and splits the bill evenly between however many people you choose.

Frequently asked questions

My receipt isn't scanning correctly.
Try scanning in good lighting with the receipt flat on a surface. Tap the shutter button only when the document outline turns solid. You can also tap "Use Photo" to pick an image from your library instead.

The subtotal or total looks wrong.
On the Review screen, tap any number to edit it manually before proceeding to the results.

What does "tipping on tax" mean?
Some restaurants pre-fill tip suggestion amounts by applying the tip percentage to the total (subtotal + tax) rather than just the subtotal. That means you're effectively tipping on your tax — a few extra dollars per meal. DipOut catches this and shows you what the tip would be on the subtotal alone.

Is my receipt data stored or uploaded?
No. All image processing happens on your device using Apple's Vision framework. Nothing is ever sent to a server. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?
On the paywall screen, tap Restore Purchase. If you bought DipOut Pro on this Apple ID, it will be restored at no charge.
