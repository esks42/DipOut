The receipt reads itself.

DipOut's OCR pulls the subtotal, tax, and total straight off the paper — no typing, no account.

Custom is to tip pre-tax.

Gratuity is customarily calculated on the meal, before sales tax. This suggestion ran 20% on $122.08 instead of $112.00 — often just the payment terminal's default setting, but you're still tipping on the tax.

Recalculated on the subtotal.

DipOut reruns the same percentage on the pre-tax amount. Same generosity, correct basis.

Two dollars tonight. Two hundred a year.

Nobody tips the tax on purpose — but at twice a week, that quiet $2.02 is about $210 a year. DipOut flags it on every receipt. And when you split, exact-cent reconciliation means no one pays the rounding error.
